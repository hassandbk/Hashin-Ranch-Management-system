**Ranch Management System**

The Ranch Management System is a comprehensive JavaFX and FXML-based application tailored to efficiently oversee and organize various aspects of a ranch. From managing diverse livestock to handling inventory, feed, breeding, and generating comprehensive reports, this system simplifies the complexities of ranch management.



**Features**

**1. Livestock Management**
Multi-species Support: Easily manage different types of livestock including sheep, chicken, goats, cattle, fish, and more.
Health Monitoring: Track and monitor the health status of each animal, facilitating timely interventions and treatments.
Productivity Tracking: Record and analyze data related to livestock productivity such as milk yield, egg production, and weight gain.

**2. Inventory Management**
Feed Inventory: Keep detailed records of feed inventory levels, ensuring adequate supply for all types of livestock.
Equipment Management: Manage equipment and supplies necessary for ranch operations, facilitating timely maintenance and replacement.
Supply Chain Integration: Seamlessly integrate with suppliers to streamline inventory replenishment processes.

**3. Feed Management**
Customized Feeding Plans: Develop and implement customized feeding plans for different types of livestock, optimizing nutrition and resource utilization.
Feed Tracking: Monitor feed consumption patterns and adjust feeding schedules accordingly to minimize waste and maximize efficiency.

**4. Breeding**
Breeding Records: Maintain comprehensive breeding records including mating dates, parentage information, and offspring details.
Breeding Programs: Plan and execute breeding programs to enhance desirable traits and genetic diversity within the livestock population.

**5. Livestock Reports**
Customizable Reports: Generate customizable reports on various aspects of ranch operations such as livestock health, productivity, breeding activities, and inventory usage.
Data Analysis: Utilize generated reports for data-driven decision-making, performance evaluation, and long-term strategic planning.

**Usage**
Installation: Clone the repository to your local machine.
Setup: Open the project in your preferred Java IDE.
Execution: Run the Main.java file to start the application.
Interface Navigation: Utilize the intuitive interface to navigate through different features and efficiently manage your ranch operations.

**Dependencies**
JavaFX: A platform for creating rich internet applications.
FXML: An XML-based markup language for defining user interfaces in JavaFX applications.
